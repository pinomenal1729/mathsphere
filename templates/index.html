I am Anupam Nigam, a 27-year-old assistant professor of mathematics passionate about making mathematics beautiful and accessible. I teach linear algebra to data science students, and mathematics to Class 11, 12, and graduation-level students.

02
My Vision

Mathematics is not a subject — it is the language of the universe. MathSphere bridges the gap between content and conceptual clarity. Every student deserves step-by-step understanding, not just answers.

What I Do

Beyond teaching, I create mathematics content on YouTube and Instagram, explore quantum computing, work on data science and machine learning, and build educational tools. Deep interests include elliptic curve cryptography and the mathematical foundations of AI.

MathSphere was built with one goal: give every student access to a brilliant mathematics teacher who never sleeps and always explains clearly. It uses state-of-the-art AI with verification to ensure accuracy — because in mathematics, accuracy is everything.
